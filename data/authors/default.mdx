**Sameer Sahasrabudhe** is a Professor of Practice in Design at IIT Gandhinagar, with over 25 years of cumulative experience in advertising, animation film-making, and educational multimedia production.

## Research & Innovation

He has contributed to the development of the [Learner-Centric MOOC (LCM) model](http://lcm-model.org) and has created many MOOCs for [IITBombayX](https://iitbombayx.in/courses/basic-3d-modeling-using-blender), [edX](https://www.edx.org/course/basic-3d-modeling-using-blender), and [SWAYAM](https://online-degree.swayam.gov.in/nd1_tqp20_c01_s2_ge01/preview).

His course on 3D visualization and animation has been subscribed by over **100,000 learners worldwide** and was shortlisted for the [edX Prize 2019](https://blog.edx.org/congratulations-2019-edx-prize-finalists/).

## Academic Background

Sameer holds a **PhD in Subject Communication**, Post Graduate Diplomas in **Distance Education** and **Animation Film Making**, and a Graduation in **Fine Arts**. He has been a member of academic councils and boards of studies for many Universities.

## Creative Work

Apart from his academic pursuits, Sameer is an accomplished filmmaker and visual designer. He has won awards at various film festivals for his documentary and animation films, and continues to explore the intersection of technology, education, and creative expression.

## Philosophy

*"I like to think of myself as an explorer of visual art forms. My journey has been about finding ways to make educational content as engaging as entertainment content."*
